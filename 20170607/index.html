Mountains are Earth’s way of reminding us of her existence.
Mountains are Earth’s intimacy with the sky.

Silly pilgrims climb Mountains in a quest for salvation.
Silly skiers descend Mountains in a quest for salvation.

Mountains are Earth’s offspring from colliding lands.
Mountains are Earth’s middle-finger to desert sands.

Silly painters draw up Mountains to produce awe.
Silly hikers hike up Mountains to consume awe.
